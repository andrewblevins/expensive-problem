Expensive Problem

A weekend workshop · July 31–August 1, 2026 · NYC

Bring your most expensive problem. Leave with clarity, energy, and a way forward.

Who is this for?

You're high-functioning, creative, and entrepreneurial. You think from left field. You live for transformation. You've had sudden breakthroughs before, and you know it can happen again. You believe it's possible to shift your perspective in a weekend.

You are confident in your capacity to take action in your personal life or work. However, right now you're stuck. It's frustrating. Annoying. It drags at your attention. You can't quite put it down. You know that if you can move through it, life will feel different. You'll be free to act, to create, and to lead.

What's an expensive problem?

It's a problem that is costing you dearly. So much that you lose focus, or can't sleep at night. The cost may be money, or something you can't put a number on—time, peace of mind, energy. Whatever it is, the price is too high to ignore.

You'll bring your expensive problem to a small group of peers and work on it together.

Example Scenarios

  • You're burning out but the company can't afford for you to slow down.
  • Your company has hit a ceiling. You know the next stage of growth requires a fundamentally different strategy, or a different version of you as a leader. You can't see which, or what that looks like.
  • You've built a company where the product is you. You'd like to exit while the company continues to thrive, but you're afraid that if you leave, the whole thing will collapse.
  • You and your cofounder are close friends and strong partners. However, you have a conflict with them that feels significant and you can't find a way to talk with them about it.
  • The company is succeeding, but you're not sure you're the right person to lead it anymore. You can't tell if that's clarity or fear.
  • You're facing a decision between paths that can't be combined. Each one means giving up something real. But staying put isn't an option either.

What you'll walk away with

On Sunday evening, you will have:

  • The ability to see your problem from multiple perspectives—adult developmental psychology, systematization and automation, structural analysis of interactional power, metarationality.
  • A way of seeing your situation that you could not have reached on your own.
  • Clarity of direction and renewed energy.
  • Concrete next steps and the confidence to take them.

This is not about abstract ideas. It's about dismantling what's keeping you stuck, and being inspired to take practical action on the other side.

How the weekend works

The workshop takes place July 31st–August 1st, 2026 in New York City. Over two days, you'll participate in four sessions in a peer group of no more than twenty participants.

You'll engage in intensive group exploration, learn frameworks you can apply immediately, and work directly on your specific problem. The process is rigorous and sometimes surprising. Spacious breaks are built in so that insights land and conversations deepen.

You will leave not only with clarity on your own problem, but with new ways of seeing that extend far beyond it.
